Gweru Ward Website Plan

Site Name: Gweru Ward - The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

Optional Domain: gweruward.org

Site Purpose

The website will provide spiritual resources, announcements, activity schedules, and contact details for Gweru Ward members and visitors. It will serve as a hub to strengthen communication, share uplifting messages, and keep members informed of upcoming events.

Scenarios

  • What time does sacrament meeting start in Gweru Ward?
  • Where can I find the contact information for the Bishopric?
  • What activities are planned for the youth this month?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2E7D32 (Green) – used for headings, navigation bar, and accents.

Secondary Color: #F5F5F5 (Light Gray) – used for background and contrast with text.

Typography

Heading Font: "Merriweather", serif – for titles and headings.

Body Font: "Open Sans", sans-serif – for general text and paragraphs.
